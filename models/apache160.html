Overview

The TVS Apache RTR 160 4V is the most powerful 160cc motorcycle in its segment. Born of Racing DNA, it features a 4-valve oil-cooled engine that delivers best-in-class performance. With SmartXonnect technology and 3 ride modes (Sport, Urban, Rain), it adapts to every road condition.

Engine & Performance

  • Engine: 159.7 cc, 4-stroke, Oil-cooled, Fuel Injection
  • Max Power: 17.55 PS @ 9250 rpm (Sport Mode)
  • Max Torque: 14.73 Nm @ 7250 rpm
  • 0-60 km/h: 4.8 seconds
  • Top Speed: 114 km/h

Chassis & Brakes

  • Frame: Double Cradle Split Synchro Stiff Frame

  • Brakes: 270mm Petal Disc (Front) / 200mm Disc or Drum (Rear)
