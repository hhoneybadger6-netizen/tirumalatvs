TVS NTORQ 150

India’s most powerful performance scooter built for thrill, technology and sporty riding experience

Overview

TVS NTORQ 150 is a high-performance sporty scooter designed for riders who want aggressive styling, strong acceleration and advanced connected technology. It builds on the NTORQ DNA with enhanced power, superior handling and race-inspired design.

Key Highlights

  • Most powerful TVS scooter with class-leading performance
  • Aggressive race-inspired design and sporty stance
  • Advanced SmartXonnect connected technology
  • Excellent acceleration and handling stability
  • Trusted TVS build quality and reliability

Engine & Performance

  • Engine: 149.5 cc, liquid-cooled engine
  • Power: Approx. 15 PS
  • Torque: Approx. 13.5 Nm
  • Transmission: Automatic (CVT)
  • Ride Focus: Sporty acceleration and stable cornering

Variants Available

  • TVS NTORQ 150 Standard
  • TVS NTORQ 150 Race Edition

Colour Options

  • Race Red
  • Stealth Black
  • Electric Blue
  • Matte Grey

Customer Reviews (Summary)

  • Highly praised for power and sporty performance
  • Excellent ride stability at higher speeds
  • SmartXonnect features appreciated by young riders
  • Premium feel and solid road presence

Why Choose TVS NTORQ 150?

TVS NTORQ 150 is the perfect choice for riders who want a scooter that delivers motorcycle-like performance with the convenience of an automatic transmission. It is ideal for enthusiasts seeking speed, technology and style in one powerful package.
