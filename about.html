Our Legacy

Trust, Value, and Service since 1911

The TVS Story

More than just a manufacturer, we are a tradition of trust.

TVS Motor Company is a reputed two and three-wheeler manufacturer globally, championing progress through sustainable mobility. We are the flagship company of the USD 8.5 billion TVS Group, which has a heritage of over 100 years. Rooted in our 100-year legacy of Trust, Value, and Passion for Customers and Exactness, we take pride in making internationally aspirational products of the highest quality through innovative and sustainable processes.

The story began in 1911, when our founder, T.V. Sundaram Iyengar, established a bus service in Madurai. He was a visionary who believed that the foundation of any business should be laid on the solid ground of ethics and customer satisfaction. This belief has been passed down through generations, evolving into the core philosophy of the TVS Group: Trust, Value, and Service. Today, we are the only two-wheeler manufacturer to have received the prestigious Deming Application Prize, the highest international award for Total Quality Management.

"The Racing DNA"

Racing is not just a sport for us; it is in our DNA. TVS Racing, established in 1982, has been the pioneer of motorsports in India. With over 40 years of racing heritage, we have consistently dominated the tracks, winning unmatched championships. This intense experience on the track fuels the engineering of our production vehicles. The TVS Apache series and the TVS Ntorq are direct descendants of this racing pedigree, bringing track-tuned performance to your daily ride.

Innovation & Technology

At TVS, innovation is a way of life. From launching India's first 2-seater moped to introducing the revolutionary RT-Fi (Race Tuned Fuel Injection) technology, we have always been at the forefront of engineering excellence. Our collaboration with BMW Motorrad has resulted in world-class 310cc motorcycles that are celebrated globally. Furthermore, our leap into the future with the TVS iQube Electric showcases our commitment to a greener, sustainable future without compromising on performance.

Tirumala TVS: Your Trusted Partner in Bangalore

Tirumala TVS is proud to represent this glorious legacy in Bidarahalli, Bangalore. As an authorized main dealer, we do not just sell vehicles; we build relationships. Our state-of-the-art showroom allows you to experience the full range of TVS vehicles in a comfortable environment. Our service center is staffed with factory-trained technicians who treat your vehicle with the care it deserves, using only genuine TVS parts.

We understand that buying a two-wheeler is a significant milestone for every Indian family. Whether you are a college student looking for the thrill of an Apache, a family man seeking the comfort of a Jupiter, or a professional switching to the smart efficiency of the iQube, Tirumala TVS is here to guide you. We promise transparent pricing, quick finance options, and an after-sales experience that keeps you smiling for miles.
