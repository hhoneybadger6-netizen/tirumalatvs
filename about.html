Welcome to Tirumala TVS

Located in Bidarahalli, Bangalore, we are proud to represent TVS Motor Company. Our mission is to bring the world-class engineering of TVS to your doorstep with unmatched customer service, transparent dealings, and a commitment to your riding joy.

A to Z of TVS Motor Company

From a bus service in 1911 to a global motorcycle giant today.

1911

The Foundation

Founded by Shri T.V. Sundaram Iyengar, the TVS Group began as a bus service in Madurai.

1980

India's First 2-Seater Moped

TVS launched the TVS 50 from its Hosur factory. It was India's first two-seater moped.

1982

Birth of TVS Racing

TVS established TVS Racing, the first factory racing team in India.

2002

The Deming Prize

TVS Motor Company became the first two-wheeler company in the world to be awarded the Deming Prize.

2013

BMW Partnership

TVS signed a long-term strategic partnership with BMW Motorrad.

2020

Acquisition of Norton

TVS acquired the iconic British brand Norton Motorcycles.
