A to Z of TVS Motor Company

From a bus service in 1911 to a global motorcycle giant today.

1911

The Foundation

Founded by Shri T.V. Sundaram Iyengar, the TVS Group began as a bus service in Madurai. It became synonymous with punctuality ("You can set your watch by a TVS bus").

1980

India's First 2-Seater Moped

TVS launched the TVS 50 from its Hosur factory. It was India's first two-seater moped and revolutionized personal transport for the common man.

1982

Birth of TVS Racing

TVS established TVS Racing, the first factory racing team in India. This racing DNA is now infused into every Apache and Ntorq engine.

2002

The Deming Prize

TVS Motor Company became the first two-wheeler company in the world to be awarded the prestigious Deming Prize for Total Quality Management.

2013

BMW Partnership

TVS signed a long-term strategic partnership with BMW Motorrad. Together, they developed the world-class 310cc platform (Apache RR 310).

2020

Acquisition of Norton

TVS acquired the iconic British brand Norton Motorcycles, marking its entry into the super-premium global motorcycle market.
