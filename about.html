Welcome to Tirumala TVS

Located in the heart of Bidarahalli, Bangalore, Tirumala TVS is your premier destination for TVS two-wheelers. We are committed to delivering the "TVS Promise" of quality and customer satisfaction.

Whether you are looking for a daily commuter like the Jupiter, a racing beast like the Apache, or a sustainable electric ride like the iQube, we ensure a seamless buying experience with transparent pricing, quick finance approvals, and excellent after-sales service.

The TVS Legacy

A 100+ Year History of Innovation

1911 - The Beginning

The TVS Group was founded by Shri T.V. Sundaram Iyengar. It began as a bus service in Madurai and grew into one of India's largest automotive conglomerates, built on the values of Trust, Value, and Service.

1980 - India's First 2-Seater Moped

TVS launched the TVS 50, Indiaâ€™s first two-seater moped. It revolutionized personal mobility in India, becoming a household name for durability and affordability.

The Racing DNA

TVS Racing (est. 1982) is the first factory racing team in India. This racing experience directly influences the engineering of the Apache Series, bringing track performance to the road.

Global Partnerships

TVS Motor Company has a strategic partnership with BMW Motorrad (manufacturing the G310R and Apache RR310). In 2020, TVS acquired the iconic British brand, Norton Motorcycles.

Quality Excellence

TVS Motor is the only two-wheeler manufacturer in the world to be awarded the prestigious Deming Prize for Total Quality Management (TQM).

Future Mobility

With the launch of the TVS iQube and the premium TVS X, the company is leading the charge in sustainable, electric urban mobility.
